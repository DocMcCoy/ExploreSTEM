IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTE: For the safety of participants in ALL sessions, ExploreSTEM will be strictly enforcing the following:

  • participants must wear long pants;
  • wear closed-footed shoes (no flip flops or sandals);
  • tie back long hair;
  • wear a lab coat and safety glasses (if provided) and;
  • NO eating or drinking in the labs.

Failure to comply will result in students not being permitted to participate
